Meet Your Instructor

Be Prepared

What to Bring

👟

Footwear

Closed-toe shoes or boots with a small heel. No sandals, flip flops, or open-toe shoes around horses.

👖

Clothing

Long pants are recommended for riding (jeans or leggings). Layer up — Pacifica mornings can be foggy and cool!

🥙

Packed Lunch

Bring a hearty packed lunch and plenty of water. Ranch life works up an appetite!

🧴

Sunscreen & Hat

Apply sunscreen before camp. Bring a baseball cap or wide-brimmed hat for outdoor time.

Provided: Riding helmets, grooming supplies, and all horse-related equipment.
